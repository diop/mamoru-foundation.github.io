# Getting Started
Welcome to the "Getting Started" page of the Mamoru documentation! We're excited to help you begin your journey with Mamoru, a powerful blockchain-native exploit monitoring and prevention platform designed to secure the rapidly evolving blockchain and Web3 ecosystem. This guide will walk you through the essentials of setting up and using Mamoru to protect your projects and transactions from potential threats.

Whether you're a developer, blockchain enthusiast, or an organization seeking to enhance your security infrastructure, Mamoru offers a comprehensive and scalable solution to safeguard your interests in the world of decentralized finance (DeFi), crypto-exchanges, and beyond.

In the following sections, we'll cover the key concepts and components of Mamoru, as well as guide you through the steps to start utilizing the platform for your security needs:

1. **System Architecture**: Understand the architectural design of Mamoru, its components, and how they work together to provide a secure and robust monitoring system.
2. **Supported Networks**: Learn about the various blockchain networks supported by Mamoru and how to integrate them with the platform.
3. **Monitoring and Detection**: Explore Mamoru's powerful monitoring capabilities and how to use its customizable rules engine to detect potential threats and anomalies in real-time.
4. **Incident Response**: Discover how Mamoru's incident response mechanism works to effectively address and mitigate security risks.

Let's embark on this exciting journey together and unlock the full potential of Mamoru to create a safer and more secure blockchain ecosystem!
